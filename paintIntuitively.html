Paint Intuitively

2022
performance An artist Yukyung Chung (RISD MFA, Digital + Media, 2023) responds to her composed soundscape through live painting. Chung's artistic practice explores intuitive creative expression in contrast to an intentional, controlled approach. Following Chung's painting session, hear about her process and discuss the results. The artist is documenting the painting portion in the garden.
