Nocturne op.9 No.2

2022
Single-channel video, HD video, color, sound, 4â€™27
In the work , she reinterprets existing classic music(piano) in three movements. The three movements are all meaningful in building up the body with exercise, gymnastics, and facial massage. Each movement of the three-body performance is substituted into the 88 piano notes. Just as the piano notes are played according to the notation, my movements are also played in a set order. Just as music is played by the rule of notation, three movements exist by implicit rules. Through this process, she wants to talk about the implicit stereotype of beauty created by society. She expresses the appearance of humans operating according to the given rules like a machine doing a given task through her movements.
