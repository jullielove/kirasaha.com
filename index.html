Yukyung Chung is a multidisciplinary artist and art director based in New York and South Korea. Her practice encompasses digital media, technology, sonic experimentation, painting, and performance. With a BFA in painting from the University of Kyung Hee and an MFA in Digital+Media from the Rhode Island School of Design, she combines traditional and contemporary approaches to create innovative works. Her work explores the intersection of physical movement and technology, incorporating new technologies based on her gestures and movements.
